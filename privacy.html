Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

The short version: Real IQ does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no ads, no trackers, and no analytics. Everything stays on your device.

Information we collect

None. The app collects no personal data. We operate no servers and never receive any information from your device. Real IQ works completely offline.

Data stored on your device

So you can see your history, the app stores your test results (scores and the date taken) locally on your device only. This information never leaves your device, is never transmitted to us or anyone else, and is removed if you delete the app.

Third parties

The app contains no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking software, and does not make any network connections. Nothing you do in the app is shared with anyone.

Children's privacy

Because the app collects no personal information and uses no advertising or analytics, it does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the "last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email brandonhalterman@gmail.com.

Real IQ provides an estimate of reasoning ability for educational and self-assessment purposes. It is not a clinically administered IQ test and is not a substitute for professional psychological assessment or diagnosis.
