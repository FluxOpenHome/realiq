How the test works

Real IQ presents a series of reasoning problems — abstract matrix patterns plus numerical, verbal, and spatial items — and measures how many you solve and how quickly. Your performance is mapped onto the standard 100/15 scale and shown with your percentile and classification.

Frequently asked

Is this a real IQ score?

It's an estimate of your reasoning ability based on the same kinds of problems used in real intelligence tests, scored on the standard scale. It is not a clinically administered test and is not a diagnosis.

Why is the test different every time?

Every problem is generated fresh, so the test can't be memorized — which keeps repeat results meaningful.

Do I need an internet connection?

No. The app works completely offline.

Is my data private?

Yes. The app collects no personal information and makes no network connections. Your results stay on your device. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I clear my results?

Deleting and reinstalling the app removes all locally stored results.
